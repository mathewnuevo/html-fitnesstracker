Workouts

Jumping Jacks

Description: A full-body exercise that increases heart rate and burns calories.

Steps:

  • Stand with feet together and arms at sides.
  • Jump up, spreading legs and raising arms overhead.
  • Jump back to the starting position.

Squats

Description: A lower body exercise that strengthens legs and core.

Steps:

  • Stand with feet shoulder-width apart.
  • Lower hips back and down while keeping your chest up.
  • Push through your heels to return to standing.
